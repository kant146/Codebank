]]> There are hundreds of ways that can help you in building website conversion, but we will guide you to the most prior one. Landing page. Or, “lead-capture pages” as it is often called, is where most of your potential customers drop by. It is most useful for website conversion.

Quality, not Quantity

Only deliver the critical information your viewers will need to proceed with the page. Limit the quantity and increase the quality of your content so that your visitors scan through the page fast but understand it all the way through.

Effective Headlines

Headlines are the attention grabbers. Make sure that your headlines on the landing page speak directly to your viewers and don’t drive you away from the CTA you are providing. Indian serial entrepreneur Kunal Shah has projected this “less is more” strategy by their minimalistic headlines and banners.

Comprehensible CTA

CTAs play a huge role for your visitor as it is the navigator in your landing page. The bigger and comprehensive the CTA is, the more engaged will your visitor feel. Readability is the most intriguing factor. You need to think of everyone as your visitor; so make sure the ones with special needs of understanding a text are not left alone. There are special fonts available for dyslexic people as well as font sizes.

Persuasive Questionnaire

Ask only the needful. The visitor grows impatient when they are asked things that are not related to the headlines. Don’t forget to highlight the value of the service you are offering, and put as many questions as you can, but make sure to put the needed ones only.

Appear Shorter

Muller-Lyer Illusion or the psychological technique where you can make the subject appear shorter than its real size or height. With this optical illusion channeled onto your landing page making, it ensures the visitors see the landing page as short as they want it to be. For instance, you should not leave a lot of space in between the fields. It will make your visitor’s eyes congested.

Relevant Media

Usage of to-the-point media that entails your headlines is the key to making your visitors stay on your page and increase the leads. Don’t forget to highlight the value of the service you are offering, and reduce the amount of extravagant media on the page.

Decent Layouts

Design the layout of your landing page in order of age. According to an analysis of 2020, over 90 percent of the internet surfers, use more mobile phones. So make sure you have all the layouts (i.e. desktop, mobile, tab) ready beforehand. So that the visitor faces zero inconveniences.

Encouragement

Eliminate all confusion. To keep up with the visitor’s trust, you need to be careful to provide the offer your headline directed them to your landing page. Make sure your landing page compellingly reflects on the service provided the visitors are looking for.

Better Landing Page = Better Website Conversion

The above elements are essentials for designing an effective landing page. And once that is done, we recommend you to always keep improvising. Improvising your landing page will serve you the best results.

Landing pages can feel prodigious to create but the result is what will speak.

]]> Bringing science to the betterment of society is what Cadbury has been doing since last Diwali. ‘NotJustACadburyAd’ Campaign this year too melted the hearts of millions and brought tears to our eyes. We witnessed Cadbury take a purge in sharing not just sweets but also smiles with absolutely everyone across the country!

Celebrating With the People!

The COVID-19 pandemic has been disastrous to the small businessmen and women of India. With over 6 crores of small businesses breaking down in a Keynesian tailspin fashion, the base of their optimism was shaken to the core. Diwali seemed no bright last year. Just then, like a rift in a dark cave, released Cadbury their ‘most generous ad, and called it ‘NotJustACadburyAd’.

It featured actors who promoted the names of the local shops which could be adhered to the products shown in the ad. Many small shops found the strategy life-saving as the lockdowns were increasingly taking place in the country. Nobody thought the prediction of the Second Wave will crush India’s shores so much so that the periodical lockdown would become a necessity.

This year, Cadbury released another special ad featuring a very special face and represented a very special way of celebrating Diwali 2021.

How Can You Make Your Ad Featuring The One and Only?

It’s very simple. All you have to do is go to their website and fill in the whereabouts of your shop and it will create you a hyper-personalized video where “India’s biggest brand ambassador” Shah Rukh Khan will promote your local shop. The promotional video will then be sent to the users’ WhatsApp in about 30 minutes! Impressive, right?

Stunning Concept But How Was It Designed?

As it is nearly impossible for the actor and the production to feature every single shop and make different promotional videos for each shop owner, technology lifts the sword! With the help of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, the actor’s voice and facial movements were recreated to promote the names of the local stores. The algorithm narrows down by the Pin Code of viewers watching and shows them the shops that are only nearby.

A Very Sweet Note!

The initiative to promote local stores that get no representation in the digital world for the lack of resources is a great step forward towards humanity! This Diwali, Cadbury Celebration advertises effortlessly with their thoughtful campaign and wins our hearts, yet again.
